01 · Executive Summary

From budget overruns to a Construction Operating System

NIXORA AI helps construction companies prevent budget overruns, reduce project risk, and improve profitability by turning the documents they already have — invoices, POs, PDFs, spreadsheets — into live financial and operational intelligence, with an AI copilot answering plain-language questions like "which projects are over budget?"

We propose building this in five evolving releases, each independently valuable and shippable, on a shared multi-tenant platform with billing, per-seat licensing, AI-usage metering, and audit engineered in from day one — the commercial machinery that turns a great product into a great business.

06 · Monetization & Billing Engine

We build the engine — you choose the model

We engineer the full commercial machinery — subscriptions, per-seat licensing, AI-usage metering, limit enforcement, invoicing and audit. How you price and package it is your commercial decision; the platform supports whichever model NIXORA chooses, and these are the key open questions to settle together.

Per-seat licensing

Base plan + priced seats, role-aware. The engine enforces seat counts; tier & price points are yours to set.

AI-usage credits

Meter each AI action (parse, copilot answer, forecast, vision) as billable units. You decide inclusions, overage & pack pricing.

Limit enforcement

Real-time usage meters per org with soft caps (notify) and hard caps (block / auto-purchase) — configurable.

Open questions for NIXORA

  • Per-seat vs flat vs hybrid pricing — and your target ARPU?
  • How many AI credits bundled per plan, and overage behaviour?
  • Top-up packs & volume discounts — sold, or usage-billed monthly?
  • Free trial / freemium tier, or sales-led only?
  • Annual vs monthly billing & discount strategy?
